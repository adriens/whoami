I first discovered schemacrawler as a standard end user. I've been a dba for many years (essentially Oracle and postgresql) and went back to development. I manage development and maintenance operations with devops philosophy and I wanted to be able to:

1. Report database in a graphical way, with open source software (that argument was very important for me)
2. Be able to perform grep-like operations on all objects
3. Perform reporting (grants, types, sizes, ...) on all database objects
4. Last but not least be able to report lint and, most important to be able to implement mines

I did find schemacrawler, began to use it and I could be very efficient, very fast and had only good surprises with this excellent software that was doing the job exactly the way I wish, it was as if someone else did develop the software for me!

I also began to have some more custom needs, essentially:

1. Create "extensions" to schemacrawler to create my own reports (also called plugins)
2. Create custom lint

First I wanted to be grateful to Sualeh for his great job, and as I'm working on an Ubuntu derivative, I decided on my own to create a .deb installer, totally maven driven that refactors the install process, including drivers, command lines, etc... Once the job was done, I decided to send a mail to Sualeh to tell him that this was now available. That was the starting point of our collaboration!

His feedback did arrive within the same day I sent my mail. Very kind and professional answer. He found my idea very interesting and we had a discussion on schemacrawler distribution which has now changed. He also made me proposals on my debian package, proposals that I did adopt.

We did never meet but collaboration and software vision and passion could be easily shared and it's a real pleasure and proudness to collaborate with Sualeh. I love his software and the way he did conceive his software which can be extended in a very easy way. He is very opened to new ideas, improvements, and, if interesting, does implement them in a very fast way. Team collaboration with Sualeh is a very nice experience. Furthermore, he's very detail oriented and the finished product does show that.

I'm very grateful to Sualeh for sharing his work, experience and expertise with me, and hope that we'll be able to go on to collaborate.

Thank you Sualeh.
