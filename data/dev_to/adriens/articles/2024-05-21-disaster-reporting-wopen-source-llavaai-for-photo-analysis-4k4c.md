My country recently faced severe events: 

[![Image description](https://dev-to-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/articles/b5hyr0lwvh1z6x8ztiti.png)](https://www.dnc.nc/dossier-retour-en-images-sur-une-semaine-de-chaos/)

## 🤔 Inception

These events generated a lot of visual contents, especially photos at an amazing pace and from a very wide variety of media sources.

These contents generated a lot of emotions while we were lacking of sleep. Still we need to be able to perform analysis.

**The purpose of this post is to show a way to analyze these contents, with Open Source software... and what we can learn from this technology in a context of social crisis and emotion overwhelm.**
