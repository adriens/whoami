## 🍿 Enjoy the slideshow

I have implemented the Slideshow with [reveal.js](https://github.com/hakimel/reveal.js/) so people can enjoy it on a wide variety of devices.

> For each president I've asked to `DALL-E mini` to create me the picture of a "hand made president".

## 📑 USA Presidents indexed

The slideshow is organized in a way it makes it possible to get the n.th president by it's position.

👉 For exeample, to get :

- The `first` president : simply [`dall-e-mini-hand-made-usa-presidents/1`](https://adriens.github.io/dall-e-mini-hand-made-usa-presidents/#/1)
- Thomas Jefferson is the `third` president, so : [`dall-e-mini-hand-made-usa-presidents/3`](https://adriens.github.io/dall-e-mini-hand-made-usa-presidents/#/3)
- [Abraham Lincoln](https://adriens.github.io/dall-e-mini-hand-made-usa-presidents/#/16)
- Some surprise on the [20 th.](https://adriens.github.io/dall-e-mini-hand-made-usa-presidents/#/20)
- [F. Roosevelt looking awesome](https://adriens.github.io/dall-e-mini-hand-made-usa-presidents/#/32)
- Fantastic looking [R. Nixon](https://adriens.github.io/dall-e-mini-hand-made-usa-presidents/#/37)
