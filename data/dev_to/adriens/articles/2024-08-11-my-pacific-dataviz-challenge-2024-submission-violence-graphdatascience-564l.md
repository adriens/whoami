## 🤩 What I learnt

While preparing the data, I learnt a lot about : 

- **Socio-professional** categories : [`PCS 2020`](https://www.insee.fr/fr/metadonnees/pcs2020/groupeSocioprofessionnel/1?champRecherche=true) from [INSEE.fr](https://www.insee.fr/fr/information/2406153)
- **Violence categorization** thanks to [The Council of Europe Convention](https://www.coe.int/en/web/gender-matters/council-of-europe-convention-on-preventing-and-combating-violence-against-women-and-domestic-violence)

**👉 I could enhance the dataset with these to perform meaningful and standardized aggregations.**

So I decided to explain this in a dedicated _"behind the scene dataprep"_ where I explain what I learnt, did and the benefits I got out of these news data.
