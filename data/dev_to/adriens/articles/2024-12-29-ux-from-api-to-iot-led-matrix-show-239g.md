## 💭 About Open Innovation, students and side projects

Since a few years now I love to make projects in parternship between [OPT-NC](https://x.com/OPT_NC) and students at [UNC](https://****x.com/unc_nc) (University of New-Caledonia).

It uses **freely available data and - sometimes beta - APIs**... and is managed as a personal side project in free time (week-ends and evenings).

This is the kind of art that triggered this project's inception : 

> _What if we were building such a useful led-based artefact that would produce in a mean time **a nice looking & cool artistic performance while delivering useful live information** thanks to minimalist dataviz ?_
