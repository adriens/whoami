## ❔ About

As [Trivy](https://trivy.dev/) is becoming quite popular : 

{% twitter 1901655379542475129 %}

I decided to give it a first try... and see **what could be achieved within a very (very) few lines of code** : 

- With datascience
- Scheduled on Kaggle
- With existing datasets
- Deliver nice ready-to-use data as [`duckdb`](https://duckdb.org/), [`parquet`](https://parquet.apache.org/) and `csv`

... and last but not least produce some first dataviz and see how it goes:


[![Image description](https://dev-to-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/articles/z4p8804xim4rr5tbwx7i.png)](https://www.kaggle.com/code/optnouvellecaldonie/opt-nc-dockerhub-trivy-scans-for-dummies)


But... enough talk, let's see how it looks like !
