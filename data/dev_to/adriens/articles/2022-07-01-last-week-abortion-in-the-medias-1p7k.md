## ✂️ Get & Build videos

```
mkdir abortion && cd abortion
yt-dlp https://www.youtube.com/results\?search_query\=abortion\&sp\=EgYIAhABGAM%253D \
    --write-auto-sub
videogrep --input *.webm --search 'abortion' --resyncsubs 0.1 \
    --output abortion_sentence.mp4
videogrep --input *.webm --search 'abortion' \
    --search-type fragment --resyncsubs 0.1 \
    --output abortion_shorts.mp4
```
