## 🤔 About _Women's empowerment_

The "fun fact" is that since this data collection I started to focus on the [Women's empowerment](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Women%27s_empowerment):

> _"Overall, woman empowerment aims to create a more equitable and inclusive society where women have the agency, opportunities, and support needed to realize their full potential and contribute to the development of their communities and societies."_

Which covers many areas like:

- Education
- Economic Independence
- Healthcare
- Legal Rights
- Political Participation
- Social and Cultural Change

**👉 So I decided to create this dedicated series to learn and share on this topic, with datascience tools.**
