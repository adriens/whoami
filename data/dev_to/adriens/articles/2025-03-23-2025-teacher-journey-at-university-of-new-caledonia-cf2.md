## 🎯 (Student )Learning Objectives 

As detailed in the [provided syllabus](https://github.com/adriens/unc_iae_miage_interop_si_feedbacks/blob/main/SYLLABUS.md#-objectifs-p%C3%A9dagogiques), here are the key objectives : 

- **Master the fundamental concepts** of interoperability: levels, challenges, and issues.  
- **Understand key technologies** and standards ensuring interoperability: protocols, data formats, ontologies
- **Implement interoperability** solutions in real-world projects. 
- **Analyze the organizational** and technical challenges related to interoperability
