## ❔ About

Recently on our K8S journey I started to use ArgoCD to deploy (HELM packaged) applications to K8S.
Quite naturally, we choose [`Argo CD`](https://argo-cd.readthedocs.io/en/stable/).

Still, while adding a new tool to our stack, I wanted to **stay on track with maintenance concerns** (with automation thanks to [API](https://endoflife.date/docs/api)).

By this I mean:

- 🛡️ Security
- 🛍️ Features
- 🗓️ End of Life (LTS, ...)

👉 Therefore I **contributed a [`[new product]`](https://github.com/endoflife-date/endoflife.date/labels/new-product)** to [`endoflie.date`](https://endoflife.date/):
