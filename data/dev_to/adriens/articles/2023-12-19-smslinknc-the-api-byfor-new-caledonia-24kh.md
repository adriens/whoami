## 🍿 Demos

SMSlink platforms provides [three services](https://smslink.nc/#services):

1. **SMS campaigns** (_marketing, customer engagement, alerts, support, and promotions, leveraging the immediacy of mobile communication_)
2. **OTP** (_enhances security by providing a temporary code for two-factor authentication, safeguarding online accounts and transactions_)
3. **SMS API** (_useful for seamlessly integrating and automating SMS communication into applications, enabling personalized messages, scalability, and real-time interaction._)
