## ☝️ About

In my daily life, I ask for **nicely formated `.csv`** files... I mean... with `,` (aka. `comma`) as separator... kindly reminding people that:

> `.csv` stands for **Comma-Separated Values** (... and that `;` mean semi-colon 🤓)

... and that respecting this default... **brings a lot of goodies**, including being able to **_"See your CSVs"_** on many systems **without additional effort.**...
