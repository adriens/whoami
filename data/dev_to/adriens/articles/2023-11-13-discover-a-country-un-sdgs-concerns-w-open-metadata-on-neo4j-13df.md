1. **💡 Brainstorm with many creative AIs** and build a project roadmap thanks to Self-Critique LLM Chain Using [LangChain](https://www.langchain.com/) & [`SmartLLMChain`](https://api.python.langchain.com/en/latest/smart_llm/langchain_experimental.smart_llm.base.SmartLLMChain.html)

## 🧑‍🎨 Data art contemplation 🎶

The creation pipeline is as follows:

1. **Export** data from [`Neo4J Bloom`](https://neo4j.com/product/bloom/)
2. **Import** data into [`Gephi`](https://twitter.com/gephi)
3. **Tune** a dataviz with Gephi (Force Atlas based)
4. **Inject** dataviz into [`Runway` Gen2](https://runwayml.com/ai-magic-tools/gen-2/)
5. **Play** with Slow motion
6. Add **soundtrack**
